Rules & Eligibility
  • Teams of 2–4 members. Solo entries accepted for Student track only.
  • Open to K-12 students, college students, and working professionals worldwide.
  • All solutions must be original — no pre-existing products allowed.
  • Final submission must include: demo video (max 5 min), source code, and a written report.
  • AI tools and APIs are permitted; judges evaluate innovation, not just tooling.
  • All submitted solutions become eligible for the ET Innovation Showcase in 2026.
